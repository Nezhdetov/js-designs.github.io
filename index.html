About me

I am a naturally inquisitive person.
I get excited by the thrill of solving a problem and coming up with ideas that can change the world!

I remember asking my parents, who are both working within the construction field as construction engineers, questions about the buildings around. I was amazed about the ability of human mind to transform the bare earth into a beautiful piece of Architecture. Ever since then I am driven by thirst for knowledge.. and I get excited by the thrill of solving a problem and coming up with ideas that are changing the tangible world that we inhabit. My career Aspiration is derives from my desire to work with that part of society that is leaving a mark behind; a legacy to show the culture of the people lived for the future generations.
